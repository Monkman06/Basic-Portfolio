About Me

  • Work History
    I am currently looking to change careers. For the last few years, I
    worked as a personal trainer at several gyms in New Jersey and
    New York City. Initially, fitness was a rewarding field; eventually
    I became tired of seeing my clients treated like nothing more than
    a spreadsheet cell by bureaucrats. Upper management willfully
    ignored reality in favor of blind adherence to unilateral policy;
    the last straw was when cooperate cared more about low sales
    than the health department raiding the gym.
  • Education
    I graduated from high school in 2005 and graduated from college
    in 2008 & 2010. I have an Associates of Science Degree in
    CriminalJustice from County College of Morris and a Bachelors
    of Arts in History from the University of Maine in Orono. In five
    years of college I only missed only one class and that was because
    I was still memorizing the schedule. I also have two primary
    personal trainer certifications and four specialties.
  • Personal/ Miscellaneous
    I am an introverted and very private person. One of my proudest
    accomplishments is losing 100 pounds; it took 15 months to lose
    and I have kept it off since April 2006. Some of my interests
    include playing chess, reading history, solving crossword puzzles,
    and playing airsoft.
